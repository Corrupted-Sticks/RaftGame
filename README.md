This is the repository for our GDW 6 game Same Day Shipping.

While cramming in our last features we expected bugs to be present, and we would iron them out during the weeks of polishing we have for the game.

However, at the last minute as we finally set up our ragdoll system, and our multiplayer, the player movement became EXTREMELY buggy. The game currently might not be very playable. We apologize for this, and are working on getting it fixed ASAP.




Same day shipping is a game where the player must ship packages by boat over the ocean to different islands, 
with weather and other events trying to hinder them. 

Controls and basic tutorial:
W/A/S/D movement                       2nd player uses Left joystick on controller.

Mouse to turn camera
 
E to interact                          2nd player uses northmost face button on controller.

F to Disembark/ Reembark(when near dock)

M to open map.


Take control of the boat by approaching the Sail and pressing E. find a dock, and walk to the end to open your job menu.

Accept a job by clicking on it from the left, and clicking Accept.

Guide the cargo from the job to the destination (Red arrow) while making sure it doesn't fall off the boat! The white arrow is the Wind direction, and will affect how fast you can go based on the sail's direction.

You can buy upgrades for the boat with the money earned from completing jobs by clicking the “Shop” button in the job menu at a dock.
